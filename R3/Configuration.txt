Conditional Matching Configuration
==================================
Using standard numbered ACL with "distribute-list"
Only 172.16.10.0/24 and 172.16.30.0/24 pass. 172.16.20.0/24 is dropped from the outbound update to R3.
R2(config)#access-list 10 permit 172.16.10.0 0.0.0.255
R2(config)#access-list 10 permit 172.16.30.0 0.0.0.255
R2(config)#access-list 10 deny any
R2(config)#router bgp 65200
R2(config-router)#neighbor 10.23.1.2 distribute-list 10 out
R2(config-router)#end
