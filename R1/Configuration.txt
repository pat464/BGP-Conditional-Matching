BGP Configuration
=================
R1#config t
Enter configuration commands, one per line.  End with CNTL/Z.
R1(config)#router bgp 65100
R1(config-router)#neighbor 10.12.1.2 remote-as 65200
R1(config-router)#address
R1(config-router)#address-family ipv4 unicast
R1(config-router-af)#neighbor 10.12.1.2 activate
R1(config-router-af)#network 172.16.10.0 mask 255.255.255.0
R1(config-router-af)#network 172.16.20.0 mask 255.255.255.0
R1(config-router-af)#network 172.16.30.0 mask 255.255.255.0
R1(config-router-af)#end
R1#
*Mar  1 00:22:27.411: %SYS-5-CONFIG_I: Configured from console by console
R1#


R1 simply advertises all 4 networks with no filtering. It doesn't know or care which ones R2 will pass along.
